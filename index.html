-HOW TO PLAY-

  • THE SOLID AND THE DASHED LINES (THREADS) CONNECT LETTERS TO FORM TWO WORDS.
  • THE CLUE ALWAYS RELATES TO BOTH WORDS IN SOME WAY.
  • WORDS CAN START FROM EITHER END OF A THREAD.
  • UNDERLINED LETTERS ARE GIVEN AND CANNOT BE CHANGED.
  • "THROW ME A BONE" REVEALS THE DIRECTION OF BOTH WORDS.
  • SCROLL LEFT AND RIGHT TO PLAY PRIOR DAYS PUZZLES.
