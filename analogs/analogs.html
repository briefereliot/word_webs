ANALOGS

MAKE PAIRS WITH THE SAME RELATIONSHIP.

  • -HOW TO PLAY-

    • SELECT TWO ITEMS TO SWAP THEIR PLACES.
    • CREATE PAIRS OF ITEMS ALL WITH THE SAME RELATIONSHIP.
    • FOR EXAMPLE, DRY : WATER, DARK : SUNLIGHT BOTH HAVE THE RELATIONSHIP "WITHOUT".
    • BUT, DRY : WATER, SUNLIGHT : DARK IS INCORRECT BECAUSE THE RELATIONSHIPS ARE REVERSED (ORDER MATTERS).
    • "THROW ME A BONE" REVEALS ONE PAIR OF ITEMS
    • SCROLL LEFT AND RIGHT TO PLAY PRIOR DAYS PUZZLES.
    • SUBMIT YOUR OWN ANALOGS TO SUBMISSIONS@PLAYWORDWEBS.COM FOR A CHANCE TO BE FEATURED AS THE SUNDAY BONUS.
